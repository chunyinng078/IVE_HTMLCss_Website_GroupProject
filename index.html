League of Legends (a.k.a LOL) is a 5 vs 5 moba game. Players can play as a champion and use different skills to fight with enemy. Players can win the game by destorying the Nexus of the another team.

How to play:

Player have to choose a Champion first:

There are 100+ champions in this game for players to choose. You have to choose a champions that is suitable in the lane you are playing.
There is five role you can play:

  1. Top
  2. JG
  3. Mid
  4. ADC
  5. Support
Click ME to know about the champions in game!!!
(Hover on the photo to zoom it bigger.)
An example, if you want to play top lane, normally you have to choose a "Tank" or "Figher" champion. Besides of the champion have to suit you lane, you have to think about what the team needs and team balance, if there is no player play the damage role in your team, you may have to consider playing a damage type champion.

What to do in game?

Hold your lane and kill your enemy!!!

You have to stay on your lane and protect your turret on your lane and try you kill your enemy to get advantage to your team!

Support your teammate!!!

If you playing JG, you can travel around the map easily, so that you should help your teammate to kill their enemy on lane and push the lane to the enemy base!

Get resources in map!!!

Killing minions to get gold in your lane, if you playing JG, kill monster in the jungle to get gold. By getting gold, you can buy item to make yourself Stronger. Beside of getting gold, you can kill so big monster to get buff for you or your team, for example, killing the blue buff will let you make more magical attack.
